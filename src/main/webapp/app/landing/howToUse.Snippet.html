How to use Cryptonomica

Here is an overview of how to use Cryptonomica

Upload your key

  • Generate your OpenPGP key pair
  • Upload your public key to Cryptonomica web-site
  • Verify your key

Sign and verify documents

  • Sign documents using your verified key
  • Find on Cryptonomica public keys of your correspondents
  • Check their signatures

Include arbitration

  • Put arbitration agreement to contracts
  • Use online dispute resolution
  • Enforce your contracts
